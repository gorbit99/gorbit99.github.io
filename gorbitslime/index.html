GorbitSlime

An affordable SlimeVR tracking solution

Features

  • Very small! Measures only 44 x 44 x 16 mm (~1.7 x 1.7 x 0.6 in)
  • Long battery life, guaranteed 15 hours of playtime from a single charge!
  • Pushbutton operation, turning off the trackers on accident is now a thing of the past!
  • Included, personalized straps and chest harness, for the best comfort while playing!
  • BMI270 IMU on-board. 20-30 minute reset times at a budget friendly price point!
  • No extensions, keep your setup wire and tangle-free!

Shipping

Shipping is done through DHL Express. This means it should take roughly 1-2 business day to ship withing the EU, 2-3 to the immediate neighbourhood of the EU and 3-5 to the US and anywhere else. Customs and taxes are paid for by the customer.

Warranty and Returns

The products can be returned for up to 2 weeks after receiving them. In this case a return label is provided for the customer. Upon receiving the returned products, the customer is refunded their payment, not including the cost of shipment and potential customization fees.

Orders can be cancelled up until the moment they are shipped out. In this case the customer will be refunded their payment, not including potential customization fees.

The units enjoy 2 years worth of warranty from the date of arrival for any manufacturing faults or damages outside the customer's control. This includes:

  • Broken or dysfunctional electronics
  • Case damages that existed before delivery was completed
  • Incorrectly shipped parts

Warranty doesn't extend to the following situations:

  • Case damages caused by dropping or incorrectly using the trackers
  • Damages caused while the trackers are disassembled
  • Unusable parts caused by a miscommunication from the customer's side, for example, incorrectly sized straps

I reserve the right to withhold the right to a warranty replacement in cases where the fault is caused by the customer, but that aren't listed above.

Replacement parts are available for purchase, in case warranty doesn't apply.
